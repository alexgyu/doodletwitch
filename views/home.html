Welcome to twitchdoodle!

twitchdoodle allows you to draw and see other people drawing over any twitch stream

To try it out, just add any active stream name to the url

Your doodles will be visible to anyone else watching the stream through twitchdoodle

Lines are automatically erased so feel free to scribble all you want!
